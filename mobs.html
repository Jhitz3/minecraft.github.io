The Mobs of Minecraft

Passive Mobs

Minecraft has many different passive mobs. Passive mobs are peaceful mobs, meaning they won't attack the player, even when provoked.

Neutral Mobs

Similar to the passive mobs, neutral mobs don't instantly attack the player. Instead, neutral mobs will only attack the player when provoked.

Hostile Mobs

Hostile mobs are mobs that will attack the player whether or not they have been provoked.
